What are the benefits of Lakeflow Spark Declarative Pipelines

1. Declarative Processing:
	Less code(Focus on what as opposed to How)
	Auto CDC API simplifies CDC Processing
	No need for manual code to handle out of order events
2. Automatic Orchestartion
	Automatic orchestration to ensure correct order of execution
	Efficient retry of Transient Failure
3. Incremental Processing
	Incremental processing engine for materialized views
	Reduces Inefficient reprocessing for updated/new records
